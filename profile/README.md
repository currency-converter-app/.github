This app has a **built-in calculator** that lets you do arithmetic inside the converter. Multiply, divide, add — all without leaving the screen. ## Works Without Internet

Cache the latest rates once, use the app forever — even in airplane mode. The offline currency converter is essential for international travel, where roaming data is expensive and Wi-Fi is unreliable.

The app stores rates locally on your iPhone. No connection needed to convert USD to EUR, check the GBP rate, or calculate JPY amounts on the go.
